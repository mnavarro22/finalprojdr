Our Focus

We are focusing on low-income communities that have little to no access to health care and facts about pollution in their enviornment. In order to fix these problems, along with others, we decided to create an app. To learn more about the app click here.
Many times, people living in areas with poor air quality also have little to no access to proper health care. Poor air quality causes certain health risks such as asthma and heart disease.

Statistics

The data table shows different categories and reports the percentage of individuals that are uninsured in Contra Costa County as a whole as well as the City of Richmond. The percentage of Richmond residents that are uninsured are greater than the County as a whole in every single category. In the household income section, it is evident that the correlation is that as the income increases the percentage of people that are uninsured decreases (health care is expensive and many residents can’t afford it!)
