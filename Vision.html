Our App

Dr. Siri & Dr. Bixby is an app that is going to be implemented into all iOS and Android devices. This app will make it so that people with no access to health care insurance can be able to have a doctor on their hands at all times. This would allow for all lower-income communities to have a sort of doctor to ensure their health.
